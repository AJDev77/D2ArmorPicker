Return to D2ArmorPicker About D2ArmorPicker by Mijago D2ArmorPicker (or short D2AP) is a small web-app to quickly find armor that fits your desired stat requirements. It uses the armor in your vault, inventory and postmaster, calculates every possible variation and shows only those that fulfill the given requirements.

As i raided a lot and I mainly play meme builds, I wanted to be able to switch exotics and have decent stats without being a burden on my team. That's why I developed the first version of this tool in Python. After a few days I started to translate this tool into a webpage. My friends liked it, but it didn't have API access. Thus I created the first version of D2ArmorPicker, and after people started using and liking it, I started to completely rewrite it - that's Version 2!

I keep updating and improving this tool, but beware that it takes a bit, as I work on it on my spare time. Feel free to follow me on Twitter , i occasionally post updates there.   1. Login. You already did this!
  2. [Optional] Pick an exotic you want to use. The tool will only display results for this exotic. You can always undo this.
  3. [Optional] Select additional mods/fragments that give a bonus (or penalty). These are important if you want certain stat combinations even if you have penalties on your gear.
  4. Select the stat distribution you seek. This is the heart of D2ArmorPicker. You just click 100 recovery and it will display you builds that have 100 recovery. Note that it automatically adds stat mods if necessary.
  5. Now look to the result table. On a normal desktop it should be on the right, on smaller devices it might move below the configuration. The table lists multiple results and you can sort it by your requirements.

 Where can I grind high stat armor? There are many spots that give you mediocre armor, but for the really good builds you need the correct armor. Here I provide a list of high-stat armor sources. It may not be complete, but a good starting point. Last updated September 24, 2021.
  • You can do Corrupted Expunges for really good high stat gear (3 per week per char).
  • Focus Tier 3 Splicer Umbral Engrams (3 per week).
  • Clear Pit of Heresy Dungeon, the boss drop guarantees two stats to be 16 (or higher).
  • Farm a bunch of Spoils, go into Master Vault of Glass and buy armor at the final chest.
